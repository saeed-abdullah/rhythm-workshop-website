Biological rhythms & technology

Biological Rhythms and Technology

Biological rhythms are multiple and overlapping. They control the rise and fall of a range of physiological factors over the short, medium and long-term. This interactive workshop will bring together researchers interested in designing technologies to support biological rhythms.
